This reproduces the paper's bounded random-walk setup in simplified form.
TD(lambda) learns state-value predictions for the probability of terminating on the right.
Smaller lambda values bootstrap more strongly, while lambda=1 behaves more like Monte Carlo / Widrow-Hoff-style learning.
Our exact numbers differ a bit because we use fewer seeds/episodes and a simpler online implementation.
